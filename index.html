About Me

Hi, I’m Tantu Mandal, a Ph.D. student at the University of Alabama. My research focuses on the study of river channels using remote sensing techniques. I’m particularly interested in understanding how river systems evolve over time and how geospatial technologies like satellite imagery and GIS can help monitor and analyze these dynamic environments. Through my work, I aim to contribute to the broader understanding of fluvial processes and support sustainable river management.
